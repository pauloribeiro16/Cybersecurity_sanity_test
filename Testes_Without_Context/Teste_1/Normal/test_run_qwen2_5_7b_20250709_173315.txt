1. **Scope**: 
   - **GDPR** focuses on protecting personal data of individuals within the European Union.
   - **NIS 2 Directive** targets critical infrastructure across the EU, aiming to enhance cybersecurity.

2. **Purpose**: 
   - **GDPR** ensures the protection of personal data and privacy.
   - **NIS 2 Directive** aims to ensure a high common level of network and information security.

3. **Regulated Entities**:
   - **GDPR** applies to any organization processing personal data, regardless of location.
   - **NIS 2 Directive** specifically targets operators of essential services (OES) and digital service providers.
